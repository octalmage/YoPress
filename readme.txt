Yo your subscribers when you publish a new post!

== Description ==

Instructions:

Signup for an API key at [yoapi.justyo.co](http://yoapi.justyo.co), for "Callback URL" put in your homepage with a trailing slash.

Put your API key in the YoPress settings and hit save changes.

Then tell your visitors to Yo your website's username, or use [button.justyo.co](http://button.justyo.co) to generate a button.

Now when you publish a new post your visitors will get a Yo with the link!
